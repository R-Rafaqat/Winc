Culture Facts

Thumbs Up

In most American and European cultures, the thumbs Up is an indicator of a job well done, or "That's great"
However, be wary of using this hand sign in Australia, Greece or the Middle East as it basically means up yours" or "sit on this".

OK sign

The 'OK' sign can cause serious offence if used in Greece, Spain or Brazil as it symbolises a body orifice; if you use this symbol you are calling someone an a**hole. In Turkey it's meant to be an insult towards gay people. In some Middle Eastern countries, such as Kuwait, the 'OK' sign signifies the evil eye.

Rock On

The symbol for rockers everywhere also has another meaning in Spain, Italy and Greece, where it is used to signify that your wife is sleeping with someone else.

Head Shaking/Nodding

In most parts of the world, nodding the head symbolizes "Yes" and shaking the head side to side means "No". This is reversed in Greece, Bulgaria and India, where nodding the head up and down means "No" and shaking/tilting the head either side means "Yes".

Fingers Crossed

In vietnam, crossed fingers are said to resemble a woman's genitals, and are considered especially heinous when directed at another person specially.
